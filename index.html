Privacy Policy for Yumb

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

At Yumb, we believe mealtime should be about connection, not stress. We protect your family's data with the same care we give our own.

1. Data Collection

We collect your email for account authentication via Firebase. Data regarding food selections and rewards is stored securely to provide the core point-tracking service.

2. Parental Control

All data entry by children requires parental approval within the app dashboard.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase Security Rules to ensure your data stays private.

Questions? Reach out via our GitHub project page.
